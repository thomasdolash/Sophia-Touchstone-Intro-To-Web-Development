Plant Care Tips

Sunlight

Most indoor plants prefer indirect sunlight. Place them near a window with filtered light to avoid leaf scorch.

Watering

Check soil moisture before watering. Overwatering is a common issue â€” allow the top inch of soil to dry out before watering again.

Soil & Fertilizer

Use well-draining soil and fertilize monthly during the growing season. Avoid over-fertilizing, especially in winter.

Seasonal Care

Adjust light and water based on seasonal changes. Reduce watering in winter and move plants closer to natural light sources.
